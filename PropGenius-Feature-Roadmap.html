Category 1
🔥 High Impact — Build Next
Core features that directly replace manual work brokers do every day. Each one has a clear before/after that brokers immediately understand.
🤖
AI & Automation
4 features
AI Follow-up Autopilot
Priority 3
Automatically sends a WhatsApp message when a lead hasn't been contacted in X days. Broker sets rules once ("If no contact for 3 days, send follow-up template"). System runs it silently. Biggest deal-saver for Indian brokers who forget.
Property Match Engine
Priority 2
When a new listing is added, AI scans all existing leads and finds matches by budget, location, and property type. Shows "12 leads match this listing — notify them?" One click sends WhatsApp to all matches.
AI Negotiation Coach
Phase 3
When a lead enters "Negotiation" status, AI suggests what counter-offer to make based on listing price, buyer budget, days on market, and area comps. Not automation — smart advice shown in the sidebar panel.
Deal Probability Score
Phase 3
Live % probability a deal will close. Calculated from: days in pipeline, number of contacts made, site visit done, budget match, lead score. Updates automatically as status changes. Shows as a progress bar on each lead.
📋
Lead Generation
2 features
Lead Capture Forms
Priority 1
Generate a unique URL or embeddable widget. Broker shares on Instagram bio, WhatsApp status, or their website. Form submissions go directly into the CRM as new leads — automatically. Closes the loop from social → CRM.
AI Chat Widget
Phase 3
Embeddable WhatsApp-style chatbot that qualifies leads before handing off to broker. Asks: budget, location preference, timeline, property type. Leads arrive pre-qualified, saving broker 10–15 min per conversation.

Category 2
🏠 Listing Superpowers
Make listings do more work for the broker — better quality, wider reach, smarter pricing, professional presentation.
Shareable Buyer Portal Page
Priority 4
Each listing gets a clean branded public page: photos, floor plan, map, AI description, WhatsApp CTA button. Broker shares a link instead of a messy WhatsApp gallery. Tracks who viewed it and for how long.
Multi-Portal Auto-Publish
Priority 5
One click publishes to 99acres, MagicBricks, Housing.com via their APIs. Currently brokers manually copy-paste to each portal — 30–45 min per listing. This alone could justify the Pro subscription for most brokers.
Virtual Staging AI
Revenue
Upload a photo of an empty room → AI furnishes it with realistic furniture. Huge for new constructions and plots. Brokers in India constantly struggle with empty-room photos that fail to sell the lifestyle. Pay-per-image model.
Listing Health Score
Phase 2
Every listing gets an actionable score: "Missing photos (-20pts), no floor plan (-15pts), price 12% above market average (-10pts). Fix these to rank higher on portals." Tells broker exactly what to improve, not just a number.
Price Trend Alerts
Phase 3
"3 similar listings in your area dropped price by 8% this week. Consider revising." Pulls from public portal data. Keeps broker competitive without them having to manually monitor the market daily.
Video Script Generator
Phase 3
AI writes a 60-second script for Instagram Reels/YouTube Shorts based on the listing details. "Start with the balcony view, mention 3BHK, price, location, end with WhatsApp CTA." Brokers doing video get 3x more inquiries.
Multilingual Listings
Phase 3
One click translates listing to Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada. Massive for Tier 2 cities and NRI buyers who prefer regional language. Gemini already supports all Indian languages natively.
RERA Compliance Checker
Phase 2
Before publishing, AI checks if listing has all RERA-required disclosures: carpet area, RERA registration number, possession date, project status. Auto-flags missing info. Brokers get fined for non-compliance — this protects them.

Category 3 & 4
💬 Communication & Client Experience
Indian real estate runs on WhatsApp. These features make communication faster, smarter, and more professional — turning conversations into closed deals.
💬
Communication Tools
4 features
WhatsApp Broadcast Campaigns
Revenue
Send a listing to a curated segment of leads: "All leads with budget ₹80L–₹1.2Cr interested in 3BHK in Andheri." Like email marketing but on WhatsApp. Pay-per-message above a monthly quota.
Voice Note Transcription
Phase 3
Broker receives a WhatsApp voice note from a client. AI transcribes it, extracts key info (budget, location, timeline), and auto-creates a lead or updates an existing one. Voice is the dominant communication mode in India.
Document Manager
Phase 2
Store property documents per listing: sale deed, EC, approved plan, occupancy certificate. Share a clean link with the buyer instead of sending 10 WhatsApp photos. Critical for closing deals faster and looking professional.
Site Visit Scheduler
Phase 2
Integrated calendar — broker sets available slots, buyer picks one from a link. Auto-sends WhatsApp reminder 1 hour before the visit. Eliminates the endless "when are you free?" back and forth on WhatsApp.
🤝
Client Experience
3 features
Post-Sale Follow-up System
Phase 2
After a deal closes (lead marked "Converted"), system schedules a check-in at 30, 90, and 180 days. "How's the new home? Know anyone looking?" Referrals are the #1 source for Indian brokers but they never nurture them systematically.
NRI Client Mode
Revenue
Special flow for NRI buyers: currency conversion display, video call scheduling with timezone awareness, digital document signing, FEMA/NRI property buying guide embedded. Huge underserved segment — NRIs buy premium properties.
Referral Partner Network
Phase 4
Broker A refers a lead to Broker B (different city or specialization). System tracks the referral, the deal outcome, and the commission owed between brokers. Very common in India — zero tools exist for managing this today.

Category 5 & 6
📊 Analytics & Agency Operations
Features that give agency owners visibility into their business and tools that help larger teams operate like a well-run company.
📊
Analytics & Intelligence
4 features
Agent Performance Dashboard
Phase 2
Which agent converts the most leads? Whose leads go cold? Average deal time per agent. Conversion rate comparison. Owners need this visibility — currently invisible in every small agency. Critical for managing a team of 3+ agents.
Lead Source ROI
Phase 2
"You got 40 leads from 99acres, 12 from MagicBricks, 8 from Instagram. 99acres converted at 15%, MagicBricks at 5%, Instagram at 25%." Tells brokers exactly where to spend their ₹50,000/month portal subscription budget.
Market Pulse
Phase 4
Aggregate anonymized data across PropGenius users to show city-level trends: average price/sqft by area this month, which micro-markets are trending, how long properties are sitting. Brokers love market data — this becomes a unique moat.
Deal Probability Score
Phase 3
Live % probability that a lead will convert. Calculated from days in pipeline, contacts made, site visit completed, budget match, and AI score. Updates automatically. Helps broker prioritize which deals to focus energy on.
⚙️
Agency Operations
4 features
Commission Tracker
Phase 2
Track expected vs received commission per deal. Which deals are pending payment? Who owes what to whom? Agencies with 5+ agents lose track of this constantly. Simple table with deal → commission → status → paid date.
Inventory Pipeline (Builder)
Phase 4
For agencies that work with builders — track which units in a project are available, booked, or sold. Assign units to specific agents. Update availability in real time. Particularly valuable for pre-launch project sales teams.
Mobile App (PWA)
Phase 2
Most Indian brokers work entirely on mobile. A Progressive Web App (PWA) that works offline — add a lead from a site visit even without internet signal, syncs when back online. "Add to home screen" install, no app store needed.
Bulk Import / Migration
Phase 2
Import 500 listings or leads at once from an Excel/CSV sheet. For agencies migrating from other CRMs or starting fresh with years of data. Currently a very painful onboarding blocker that causes churn during trial period.
