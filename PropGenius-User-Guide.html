1. Getting Started

1.1 Creating Your Account

1 Visit the PropGenius AI website and click "Get Started Free"
2 Enter your Full Name, Organization/Company Name, Email, and Password
3 Click "Create Account" (or sign up with Google)
4 Check your email for a verification link and click it to activate your account
5 Log in with your credentials — you'll be taken to the Dashboard
Tip: Your organization name is used to group all your data (listings, leads, messages). Team members you add later will share the same organization.

1.2 Logging In

  1. Go to the Login page
  2. Enter your email and password, or click "Continue with Google"
  3. Click "Sign In"

1.3 Forgot Password

  1. On the login page, click "Forgot password?"
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Click "Send Reset Link"
  4. Check your email and follow the reset link

2. Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard is your home screen after logging in. It gives you a quick snapshot of your business.

2.1 Navigation

The sidebar (left panel on desktop, bottom bar on mobile) has 6 sections:

SectionWhat It Does
DashboardOverview with stats, recent leads, and recent listings
ListingsCreate, edit, and manage property listings
LeadsCRM with table view, pipeline view, and lead details
MessagesWhatsApp conversations with leads
AnalyticsCharts, stats, and conversion metrics
SettingsOrganization info, profile, and billing

2.2 Stats Cards

Four cards at the top of the dashboard show:

  • Total Listings — total count + how many created this month
  • Active Leads — total count + number of hot leads (high AI score)
  • Conversion Rate — percentage of leads converted to deals
  • Active Listings — listings currently published and active

2.3 Recent Activity

  • Recent Leads — Latest leads with their status, location preference, and time added. Click any lead to view details.
  • Recent Listings — Latest listings with title, location, price, and status. Click any listing to view details.

3. Listings Management

3.1 Viewing Your Listings

Go to Listings in the sidebar. You'll see a table of all your property listings with:

  • Property name and type
  • Price
  • City
  • Status badge (Draft, Active, Sold, Rented, Archived)
  • Views count
  • Created date

Click any row to view full listing details.

3.2 Creating a New Listing

Click the "+ New Listing" button. The listing form is a 5-step wizard:

Step 1: Basics

1 Select Property Type: Apartment, House, Villa, Plot, Commercial, or PG/Hostel
2 Select Transaction Type: Sale, Rent, or Lease
3 Enter the Price and select the Price Unit (Total, Per Sqft, or Per Month)
4 Click "Next" to continue

Step 2: Location

1 Enter the property Address (optional)
2 Enter Locality (optional but recommended for AI)
3 Enter City (required) and select State (required)
4 Enter Pincode (optional)

Step 3: Property Details

All fields on this step are optional. Fill in what you know:

FieldDescription
BedroomsNumber of bedrooms (e.g., 2, 3)
BathroomsNumber of bathrooms
Area (sqft)Total built-up area
Carpet Area (sqft)Usable carpet area
Floor NumberWhich floor the property is on
Total FloorsTotal floors in the building
FurnishingUnfurnished, Semi-Furnished, or Fully-Furnished
FacingDirection (North, South, East, West, etc.)
Age (years)Age of the property
ParkingNumber of parking spaces
AmenitiesSelect from: Swimming Pool, Gym, Garden, Security, Parking, Lift, Power Backup, etc.
Tip: The more details you provide, the better the AI-generated content will be. The AI uses all these details to write compelling descriptions.

Step 4: Images & Floor Plan

1 Click the upload area or drag-and-drop to upload property images
2 Optionally add a Floor Plan URL if you have one hosted online

Step 5: AI Content & Save

This is where PropGenius AI shines:

1 Click "Generate with AI" to auto-generate content using Google Gemini AI
2 The AI creates: Title, Description, Key Highlights, Social Media Post, SEO Title, and SEO Description
3 Review and edit any generated content as needed
4 Or skip AI and write a Title and Description manually
5 Click "Save Listing" to save as a draft
AI generates 6 pieces of content at once: property title, detailed description, highlight bullet points, social media post (ready to copy-paste to Instagram/Facebook), SEO-optimized title, and SEO meta description.

3.3 Listing Detail Page

After saving, click any listing to view its full detail page:

  • Image gallery with thumbnails
  • Description section
  • Key Highlights — AI-generated bullet points
  • Social Media Post — ready to copy and share
  • SEO Preview — how it looks in Google search results
  • Amenities list
  • Sidebar with price, status, location, specs (bedrooms, area, floor, etc.), and performance metrics (views, inquiries)

3.4 Managing Listing Status

On the listing detail page, click "Change Status" to update:

StatusMeaning
DraftSaved but not published
ActivePublished and visible

3.5 Editing & Deleting

  • Click "Edit" on any listing to return to the 5-step form with pre-filled data
  • Click "Delete" to permanently remove a listing (you'll see a confirmation dialog)

Pipeline View
Visual board with columns for each lead status. Drag and drop leads between columns to update their stage. Ideal for tracking deal progress.

4.4 CSV Import

Bulk import leads from a spreadsheet:

1 Click "Import CSV" on the Leads page
2 Upload a CSV file or paste CSV data directly
3 Preview the first 6 rows to verify the data looks correct
4 Click "Import" to add all leads at once
CSV Format: Required column: name. Optional columns: email, phone, source, budget_min, budget_max, preferred_location, notes

4.5 Lead Detail Page

Click any lead to see their full profile:

  • Contact Information — phone, email, WhatsApp, location, budget
  • AI Lead Score — AI-powered scoring (0-100) with hot/warm/cold indicator and recommended action
  • Status — current stage with quick-change option
  • Activity Timeline — chronological log of all interactions

4.6 Logging Activities

On a lead's detail page, click "Log Activity" to record:

  • Phone Call — record call notes and outcomes
  • Email — log email interactions
  • WhatsApp — note WhatsApp conversations
  • Site Visit — record property visit details
  • Note — add any general notes

Activities appear in the timeline with timestamps, helping you track the full history of each lead.

4.7 AI Lead Scoring

PropGenius AI automatically scores each lead from 0 to 100 based on:

  • Budget alignment with your listings
  • Response time and engagement
  • Source quality
  • Activity frequency

The score appears as a badge: Hot (70+) Warm (40-69) Cold (0-39)

5. WhatsApp Messaging

5.1 Chat Interface

Go to Messages in the sidebar. The interface has two panels:

  • Left panel: List of all conversations with leads (shows name, last message preview, timestamp)
  • Right panel: Active chat window with message history

5.2 Sending a Message

1 Select a lead from the conversation list on the left
2 Type your message in the input field at the bottom
3 Press Enter to send (or Shift+Enter for a new line)

5.3 Using Message Templates

Click the template icon next to the message input to use pre-built templates:

TemplateBest For
GreetingFirst contact with a new lead
Site Visit InvitationInviting a lead to view a property
Follow UpChecking in after initial contact
Price UpdateNotifying about price changes
Thank YouAfter a site visit or meeting

Templates auto-fill with the lead's name, your organization, property details, and price. Just click a template and it populates the message field — edit if needed, then send.

Tip: Templates save significant time on repetitive messages. The Greeting template is perfect for instant responses to new leads from portals like 99acres or MagicBricks.

9. Tips & Best Practices

For Listings

  • Fill in all details before generating AI content — bedrooms, area, amenities, locality. The more data you provide, the better the AI output.
  • Upload multiple high-quality images — listings with photos get significantly more inquiries.
  • Use the social media post — copy the AI-generated social post directly to your Instagram, Facebook, or WhatsApp status.
  • Keep statuses updated — mark sold/rented properties promptly to keep your dashboard accurate.

For Leads

  • Respond within 5 minutes — leads from portals (99acres, MagicBricks) expect fast responses. Use WhatsApp templates for instant replies.
  • Use the Pipeline view daily — drag leads through stages to visualize your sales funnel.
  • Log every interaction — add activities (calls, visits, notes) to build a complete history. This also helps AI score leads more accurately.
  • Import leads via CSV — if you have existing leads in Excel, export as CSV and bulk import.
  • Set follow-up dates — never miss a follow-up by setting the "Next Follow-up" date on each lead.

For Messages

  • Use templates for speed — the Greeting template is ideal for first contact, Follow Up for re-engagement.
  • Personalize after selecting a template — templates auto-fill names and property details, but adding a personal touch improves conversion.

For Analytics

  • Check the Conversion Funnel weekly — identify which stage loses the most leads and focus your efforts there.
  • Monitor lead sources — invest more in channels that bring quality leads (higher AI scores and conversion rates).
Quick Start Workflow: Create a listing → Generate AI content → Add leads from inquiries → Message them via WhatsApp → Track progress in Pipeline view → Review performance in Analytics.
